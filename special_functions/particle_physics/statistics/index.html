Particle Statistics

Compute Bose-Einstein and Fermi-Dirac integrals, in both massive ($\mu = 0$) and massless ($\mu \inR$) cases as well as normalized to a single massless bosonic degree of freedom for the massive case.

Functions

bose_einstein_normalized_massless

Equilibrium number density of massless Bose-Einstein particle with chemical potential $\mu \inR$ normalized to a massless Bose-Einstein particle.
